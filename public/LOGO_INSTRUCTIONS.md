# Logo Setup Instructions

## How to Add Your Logo

1. Save your Silsalay logo image as `logo.png` in the `public` folder
2. The logo should be a square image (recommended: 512x512px or higher)
3. The image will automatically be displayed as a round logo with a green border

## Current Logo Settings

- **Size on Desktop**: 48px x 48px (md:w-12 md:h-12)
- **Size on Mobile**: 40px x 40px (w-10 h-10)
- **Shape**: Round (rounded-full)
- **Border**: 2px green border
- **Shadow**: Medium shadow for depth
- **Hover Effect**: Scales up slightly on hover

## File Location
Place your logo at: `public/logo.png`

The logo will appear in the navbar next to the "Silsilay" text.
